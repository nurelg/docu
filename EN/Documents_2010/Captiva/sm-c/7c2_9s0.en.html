Liftgate

Removal Procedure

  1. Remove the liftgate window upper garnish molding. Refer to "Liftgate Window Upper Garnish Molding" in this section.
  2. Open the liftgate and support.
  3. Disconnect the liftgate wiring connectors.
  4. Disconnect the rear washer hose.
  5. Pull the liftgate wiring grommet, the connectors and the rear washer hose out of the rear header.
  6. Remove the liftgate struts. Refer to "Liftgate Strut"in this section.
  7. Mark the location of the liftgate hinges to the body with a grease pencil.
  8. Remove the liftgate hinge attachment bolts.
  9. Remove the liftgate from the vehicle with and assistant.



Installation Procedure

  1. Position the liftgate hinges to the marked location points on the body, with an assistant.

    2. Notice : New hinge to body nuts must be used to ensure a proper seal.

  2. Install the liftgate hinge attachment bolts.

    3. Tighten
    Tighten the liftgate hinge bolts to 25 N•m (18 lb-in).

  3. Verify the alignment of the liftgate; adjust as necessary.
  4. Install the liftgate struts. Refer to "Liftgate Strut"in this section.
  5. Install the liftgate wiring gromemet, the connctors and the hose into the rear header.
  6. Connect the liftgate wiring connectors.
  7. Connect the rear washer hose.
  8. Install the liftgate window upper garnish molding. Refer to "Liftgate Window Upper Garnish Molding" in this section.



Liftgate Strut

Removal Procedure

  1. Raise and support the liftgate in the open position.
  2. Lift up and partially remove the spring clips on the strut using a small flat-bladed tool.
  3. Remove the upper end of the strut from the ball joint.
  4. Remove the lower end of the strut from the ball joint.
  5. Remove the strut from the liftgate.



Installation Procedure

  1. Position the strut to the liftgate.
  2. Install the upper end of the strut to the ball joint. Press in to place until fully seated.
  3. Install the lower end of the strut to the ball joint. Press in to place until fully seated.
  4. Remove the support from the liftgate.
  5. Close the liftgate.



Liftgate Latch

Removal Procedure

  1. Remove the liftgate lower tirm. Refer to "Liftgate Lower Trim" in this section.
  2. Disconnect the lock rod.
  3. Disconnect the electrical connector.
  4. Remove the bolts and the liftgate latch.



Installation Procedure

  1. Connect the electrical connector.
  2. Connect the lock rod.

    3. Notice : Dissimilar metals in direct contact with each other may corrode rapidly. Make sure to use the correct fasteners to prevent premature corrosion.

  3. Secure the liftgate latch with the bolts.

    4. Tighten
    Tighten the liftgate latch bolt to 10 N•m (88 lb-in).

GENERAL DESCRIPTION AND SYSTEM OPERATION

Fuel Filler Door

The fuel filler door attaches to the fuel tank pocket on the right side of the vehicle. The door is opened by pulling on the fuel filler door remote handle located on the floor in front of the driver’s seat.

Liftgate

The liftgate can be opened by operating the switch on the driver door. Battery voltage is supplied to the liftgate actuator through the body control module (BCM). The BCM will pulse the liftgate actuator when a release input is received through the key fob, or the driver door switch.
