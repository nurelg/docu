Captiva
   

Upper Sump Replacement

Special Tools

EN-50435 Guidance Pins

For equivalent regional tools. Refer to Special Tools .

Removal Procedure
  1. Drain the engine oil. Refer to Engine Oil and Oil Filter Replacement .
  2. Remove the front pipe. Refer to Front Pipe Replacement : LNQ → LF1 → LE5 .
  3. Remove the lower sump. Refer to Sump Replacement : Lower


  4. Remove the sump to transmission bolts (1).


  5. If equipped with AWD remove the transfer case support bracket bolt (1).


  6. Remove the sump bolts (1) and sump.

Installation Procedure
  1. Clean the sealing surface of crankshaft bearing cap tie plate and the groove in the engine front cover from old gasket material, oil, dirt and grease.


  2. Install the 2 EN-50435 guide pins (1) to the sump screw bores as shown.


  3. Apply sealant (Three-Bond, 12378521) on the sealant line (1).

    4. Caution: Refer to Fastener Caution in the Preface section.



    Note: The complete installation procedure of the oil sump should be done in 10 minutes.

  4. Loosely install the sump bolts (1) in all but the guide pin locations.
  5. Remove the EN-50435 guide pins and install the remaining sump bolts.
  6. Tighten the sump bolts to 25 N·m (18 lb ft).


  7. Install the sump to transmission bolts (1), and tighten to 40 N·m (30 lb ft).
  8. Install the transfer case bolts (1) and tighten to 40 N·m (30 lb ft).
  9. Install the lower sump. Refer to Sump Replacement : Lower .
  10. Install the front pipe. Refer to Engine Oil and Oil Filter Replacement .
  11. Refill the engine oil. Refer to Engine Oil and Oil Filter Replacement .
