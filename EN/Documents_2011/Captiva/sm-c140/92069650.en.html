1-2-Reverse Clutch Plate Disassemble

Special Tools

    • J-23327 Clutch Spring Compressor
    • J-28585 Circlip Remover
    • J-23456 Booster and Clutch Pack Compressor
    • J-45184 Differential Bearing Race Installer


  1. Remove the 1-2 reverse clutch backing plate retaining ring (622) using J-28585 remover .
  2. Remove the following components:
    3. • The 1-2 reverse clutch backing plate (621)
    • The 6 1-2 reverse clutch fibre plates (620)
    • The 6 1-2 reverse clutch steel plates (619)
    • The 1-2 reverse clutch apply plate (618)


    Note: Do not bend the 1-2 reverse clutch piston return spring assembly (601).

  3. Place J-45184 installer  (1) on the centre of J-23456 compressor  (2).
  4. Place the transmission case cover (19) with the 1-2 reverse clutch piston assembly onto J-45184 installer  (1).
  5. Using J 23327-1 (3), which is the bridge part of J-23327 compressor, compress the 1-2 reverse clutch piston return spring assembly (601).
  6. Remove the 1-2 reverse clutch piston return spring retaining ring (600) using circlip pliers.
  7. Remove the pressure from J-23456 compressor  (2), remove J 23327-1 (3) and the 1-2 reverse clutch piston return spring assembly (601).
  8. Remove all tools.


    9. Warning: Wear safety glasses when using compressed air in order to prevent eye injury.

  9. While pulling upward on the piston by hand, apply compressed air into the 1-2 reverse clutch fluid passage and remove the 1-2 reverse clutch piston (603).
  10. Remove the 1-2 reverse clutch piston inner seal (602).
  11. Remove the 1-2 reverse clutch piston outer seal (604).
