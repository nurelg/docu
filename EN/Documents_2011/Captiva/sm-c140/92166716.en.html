Liftgate Adjustment



  1. Note the original position of the tailgate latch striker.
  2. Remove the striker.
  3. Loosen the hinge-to-roof panel bolts for forward or rearward adjustments.
  4. If adjusting hinge to roof panel bolts, lower the rear headliner as needed.
  5. Loosen the tailgate-to-hinge bolts for up and down adjustment.
  6. Move the tailgate within the tailgate opening to achieve the best alignment and operation.
  7. Install striker to approximately the proper position.
  8. Adjust the striker to achieve the best operation and alignment of the tailgate.
  9. Inspect the seals for proper engagement.

    10. Caution : Refer to Fastener Caution in the Preface section.

  10. Tighten the bolts when the proper adjustment is achieved. Tighten the bolts to 25 N·m (18 lb ft).
