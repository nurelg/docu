Captiva
   

Transmission Mount Replacement - Left Side

Removal Procedure
  1. Remove the battery tray. Refer to Battery Tray Replacement .
  2. Support the engine and transmission.
  3. Remove the left tyre and wheel. Refer to Tyre and Wheel Removal and Installation .
  4. Raise and support the vehicle. Refer to Lifting and Jacking the Vehicle .
  5. Remove the engine protection shield. Refer to Engine Shield Replacement .


  6. Remove the front gearbox mount to frame bolt (1).


  7. Remove the rear gearbox mount to rear gearbox mount bracket bolt (1).
  8. Lower the vehicle.


  9. Remove the left transmission mount to transmission bolts (1).


  10. Remove the left transmission mount to frame rail bolts (1).
  11. Lower the transmission enough to provide clearance for the left transmission mount.
  12. Remove the left transmission mount (2).

Installation Procedure


  1. Install the left transmission mount (2).

    2. Caution: Refer to Fastener Caution in the Preface section.

  2. Install the left transmission mount to frame rail bolts (1) and tighten to 27 N·m (20 lb ft).
  3. Raise the transmission until the transmission contacts the left transmission mount.


  4. Install the left transmission mount to transmission bolts (1) and tighten to 57 N·m (42 lb ft).
  5. Raise the vehicle.


  6. Install the rear transmission mount to rear transmission mount bracket bolt (1) and tighten to 110 N·m (81 lb ft).


  7. Install the front transmission mount to frame bolt (1) and tighten to 100 N·m (74 lb ft).
  8. Install the engine protection shield. Refer to Engine Shield Replacement .
  9. Lower the vehicle.
  10. Install the left tyre and wheel. Refer to Tyre and Wheel Removal and Installation .
  11. Remove the engine and transmission support.
  12. Install the battery tray. Refer to Battery Tray Replacement .
