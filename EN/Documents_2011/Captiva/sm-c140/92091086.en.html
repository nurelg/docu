Steering Wheel Replacement — Short Body

Special Tools

KM-210-B Steering Wheel Puller

Removal Procedure
  1. Turn the steering wheel to the straight-ahead position.

    2. Warning: The sensing and the diagnosis module (SDM) can maintain sufficient voltage to deploy the airbags and pretensioners for up to 1 minute after the ignition has been turned OFF and the fuse has been removed. If the airbags and pretensioners are not disconnected, do not begin service until one minute has been passed after disconnecting power to the SDM. Failure to do so may cause personal injury.

  2. Disconnect the negative battery cable and let the vehicle sit for 1 minute. Refer to Battery Negative Cable Disconnection and Connection .
  3. Remove the SIR module. Refer to Steering Wheel Inflatable Restraint Module Replacement .


  4. Remove the steering wheel nut.
  5. Use paint in order to place match marks on the steering column shaft and on the steering wheel.


  6. Remove the steering wheel using the KM-210-B puller .

Installation Procedure



  1. Align the match marks on the steering wheel and the steering column shaft. Turn the signal cancelling cam on the wheel to the left.
  2. Install the steering wheel.

    3. Caution: Refer to Fastener Caution in the Preface section.

  3. Install the steering wheel nut and tighten to 41 N·m (30 lb ft).
  4. Install the SIR module. Refer to Refer to Steering Wheel Inflatable Restraint Module Replacement .
  5. Connect the negative battery cable. Refer to Battery Negative Cable Disconnection and Connection .
