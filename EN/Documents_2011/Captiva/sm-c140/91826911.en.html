Captiva
   

Turn Signal Multifunction Switch Replacement — Long Body

Removal Procedure



  1. Disconnect the negative battery cable. Refer to Battery Negative Cable Disconnection and Connection .
  2. Remove the steering column covers. Refer to Steering Column Trim Cover Replacement .
  3. Remove the indicator switch by pushing in on the tabs on the top and the bottom of the switch housing.
  4. Disconnect the electrical connections from the indicator switch.

Installation Procedure


  1. Connect the electrical connections to the indicator switch.
  2. Install the indicator switch by snapping the switch into the switch housing.
  3. Install the steering column cover. Refer to Steering Column Trim Cover Replacement .
  4. Connect the negative battery cable. Refer to Battery Negative Cable Disconnection and Connection .
