Captiva
   

Camshaft Gear Replacement - LD9

Special Tools

EN-45059 Angle Meter

For equivalent regional tools, refer to Special Tools : LD9 → LE5 .

Removal Procedure

    Warning: Refer to Battery Disconnect Warning in the Preface section.



  1. Disconnect the negative battery cable.
  2. Remove the engine beautification cover.
  3. Disconnect the ignition wires from the spark plugs.
  4. Disconnect the breather hose and PCV hose from the cylinder head cover.


  5. Remove the camshaft cover. Refer to Camshaft Cover Replacement : LE5 or LE9 → LD9 .


    6. Note: Handle the camshafts carefully to prevent nicks or scratches.

  6. Remove the timing belt. Refer to Timing Belt Replacement : LD9 .
  7. While holding the intake camshaft firmly in place, remove the intake camshaft gear bolt.
  8. Remove the inlet camshaft gear.
  9. While holding the exhaust camshaft firmly in place, remove the exhaust camshaft gear bolt.
  10. Remove the exhaust camshaft gear.
  11. Remove the camshaft position (CMP) sensor.

Installation Procedure

    Caution: Refer to Fastener Caution in the Preface section.



  1. Install the CMP sensor and tighten the bolt to 8 N·m (71 lb in).


    2. Note: Handle the camshafts carefully to prevent nicks or scratches.

  2. Install the inlet camshaft gear .
  3. While holding the intake camshaft firmly in place, install a new intake camshaft gear bolt and tighten to 50 N·m (37 lb ft) plus 60 degrees and 15 degrees using EN-45059 meter .
  4. Install the exhaust camshaft gear .
  5. While holding the exhaust camshaft firmly in place, install a new exhaust camshaft gear bolt and tighten to 50 N·m (37 lb ft) plus 60 degrees and 15 degrees using EN-45059 meter .


  6. Install the timing belt. Refer to Timing Belt Replacement : LD9 .
  7. Apply a small amount of gasket sealant to the corners of the front camshaft caps and to the top of the rear cylinder head cover-to-cylinder head seal.
  8. Install the camshaft cover. Refer to Camshaft Cover Replacement : LE5 or LE9 → LD9 .


  9. Connect the breather hose and PCV hose to the cylinder head cover.
  10. Connect the ignition wires to the spark plugs.
  11. Install the engine beautification cover and tighten the nuts and bolts to 8 N·m (71 lb in).
  12. Connect the negative battery cable.
