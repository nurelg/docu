Quarter Lower Rear Trim Panel Replacement - Long Body

Removal Procedure


  1. Remove the rear seat lower bolts. Refer to Rear Seat Belt Replacement .
  2. Remove the rear compartment stow tray. Refer to Body Side Rear Stowage Compartment Replacement .
  3. Remove the screws and the rear quarter lower trim panel.

Installation Procedure

    Caution: Refer to Fastener Caution in the Preface section.



  1. Install the rear quarter lower trim panel with the screws and tighten to 2 N·m (18 lb in).
  2. Install the rear compartment stow tray. Refer to Body Side Rear Stowage Compartment Replacement .
  3. Install the rear seat lower bolts. Refer to Rear Seat Belt Replacement .
