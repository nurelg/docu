DTC B3843

Circuit Description

The HVAC control module detects exhaust gas by an Air Quality Sensor (AQS).

DTC Descriptors

This diagnostic procedure supports the following DTC:

DTC B3843 Outside Air Quality Sensor Input Voltage

Conditions for Running the DTC

Ignition is ON.

Conditions for Setting the DTC

    • The AQS feedback voltage is less than 0.05 V.
    • The AQS feedback voltage is greater than 4.79 V.

Action Taken When the DTC Sets

    • The AQS does not operate function.
    • The AQS LED is OFF.

Conditions for Clearing the DTC

    • The DTC will become history if the HVAC control module no longer detects a fault.
    • The DTC can be cleared with a scan tool.
    • Disconnecting the HVAC control module battery feed for 10 seconds.
