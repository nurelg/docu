Sill Panel Moulding Replacement - Long Body Front



Callout

Component Name

Preliminary Procedures

  1. Open the body-side doors on the side the lower rocker moulding will be serviced from.
  2. Remove the front splash guard if equipped. Refer to Splash Guard Replacement - Front : Long Body .

1

Rocker Panel Moulding Retainer Lower (Qty: 5)

Procedure

Release the 5 lower retainers using a trim-type release tool.

2

Rocker Panel Moulding Retainer Upper (Qty: 7)

Procedure

Release the 7 upper retainers using a trim-type release tool.

3

Rocker Panel Moulding Assembly

Tip
Detach the two locking tabs on the forward portion of the rocker moulding from the lower edge of the front wing wheel opening moulding.
