Sump Replacement - LD9

Removal Procedure

    Warning: Refer to Battery Disconnect Warning in the Preface section.



  1. Disconnect the negative battery cable.
  2. Remove the engine under cover.
  3. Remove the oil filter cap .
  4. Remove the sump drain plug and drain the oil into a suitable container.


  5. Remove the exhaust front pipe from the pump converter.


  6. Remove the sump flange-to-transaxle retaining bolts.
  7. Remove the transaxle-to-sump flange retaining bolt.


  8. Remove the intermediate shaft support bracket bolts from the sump and the engine block.
  9. Remove the intermediate shaft support bracket.


  10. Remove the sump retaining bolts.
  11. Remove the sump from the engine block while pushing the engine assembly forward.

Cleaning Procedure


  1. Clean the oil pan sealing surface.
  2. Clean the engine block sealing surface.
  3. Clean the sump retaining bolts.
  4. Clean the sump attaching bolt holes on the engine block.
  5. Clean the sump scraper.

Installation Procedure


    Note: Install the sump within 5 minutes after applying the liquid gasket to the sump.

  1. Coat the sump mating surface with sealant.
  2. Install the sump to the engine block.

    3. Caution: Refer to Fastener Caution in the Preface section.

  3. Install the sump retaining bolts and tighten to 10 N·m (89 lb in).


  4. Install the sump flange-to-transaxle retaining bolts and tighten to 50 N·m (37 lb ft).
  5. Install the transaxle-to-sump flange retaining bolt and tighten to 50 N·m (37 lb ft).


  6. Install the intermediate shaft support bracket and tighten the bolts to 70 N·m (52 lb ft).


  7. Install the exhaust front pipe to the pump converter and tighten the:
    8. • pump converter-to-exhaust front pipe retaining nuts to 40 N·m (30 lb ft).
    • exhaust front pipe mounting bracket bolt and nut to 40 N·m (30 lb ft).


  8. Connect the negative battery cable.
  9. Install the oil pan drain plug and tighten to 18 N·m (13 lb ft).
  10. Install the engine under cover.
  11. Refill the engine crankcase with engine oil.
