Steering Linkage Outer Track rod Replacement

Removal Procedure
  1. Raise and support the vehicle on an alignment rack. Refer to Lifting and Jacking the Vehicle .


  2. Remove the front tyre and wheel assembly. Refer to Tyre and Wheel Removal and Installation .
  3. Mark the threads on the inner track rod to aid in repositioning the adjusting nut.
  4. Remove the outer track rod nut and disconnect the outer track rod from the steering knuckle.


  5. Loosen the outer track rod adjusting nut.
  6. Remove the outer track rod from the inner track rod.

Installation Procedure


  1. Reposition the adjusting nut to the marks on the inner track rod.
  2. Install the outer track rod onto the inner track rod.


  3. Connect the outer track rod to the steering knuckle.

    4. Caution: Refer to Fastener Caution in the Preface section.

  4. Install the outer track rod nut and tighten to 50 N·m (37 lb ft).
  5. Measure the wheel alignment. Refer to Wheel Alignment Measurement .
  6. Adjust the front toe if necessary. Refer to Wheel Alignment - Steering Wheel Angle and/or Front Toe Adjustment .
  7. Tighten the adjusting nut to 60 N·m (44 lb ft).
  8. Install the front tyre and wheel assembly. Refer to Tyre and Wheel Removal and Installation .
  9. Lower the vehicle.
