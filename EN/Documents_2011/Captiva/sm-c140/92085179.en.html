Shift Linkage Adjustment

    Warning: Refer to Battery Disconnect Warning in the Preface section.



  1. Disconnect the negative battery cable.
  2. Position the transaxle into NEUTRAL.
  3. Connect the shift control cables into the shift control adjusters.
  4. Install the shift control cables to the shift control lever housing bracket.


  5. Position the gear lever into NEUTRAL.
  6. Adjust the cable by pushing the neutral lock pin. Move the shifter slightly in order to centre the neutral lock pin.


  7. Press and lock the shift control cable adjust locks.
  8. Pull the neutral lock pin to the original position.
  9. Connect the negative battery cable.
