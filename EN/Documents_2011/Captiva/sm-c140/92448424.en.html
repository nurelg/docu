Battery Positive and Negative Cable Replacement - LF1

Removal Procedure
  1. Record all of the radio station presets.


  2. Remove the body harness terminal (2) and nut (1) from the negative battery cable stud.
  3. Disconnect the battery negative cable. Refer to Battery Negative Cable Disconnection and Connection .
  4. Remove the underbonnet fuse block cover.


  5. Remove the negative battery cable nut (1) and terminal (2), from the underbonnet fuse block.


  6. Remove the battery positive cable nut (2) and cable (3) from the fuse block.
  7. Loosen the battery positive clamp nut (1) and remove the battery positive cable from the battery.


  8. Remove the starter heat shield screw (1) and the starter heat shield (2), from the starter.


  9. Remove the battery positive cable nut (1) from the starter solenoid. Remove the battery positive cable (2) from the starter.


  10. Remove the battery negative cable ground bolt (1) from the engine block.
  11. Remove the fasteners (2) securing the battery positive and negative cable to the engine.


    12. Note: Observe how the battery positive and negative cable was routed within the engine compartment.

  12. Remove the battery positive and negative cable (1) from the engine compartment.

Installation Procedure

    Caution: Refer to Fastener Caution in the Preface section.



  1. Install the battery positive cable (2) and the alternator jumper cable (3) to the starter solenoid. Tighten to the nut (1) to10 N·m (89 lb in).


  2. Install the starter heat shield (2), with the screw(1), to the starter.


  3. Install the battery negative cable bolt (1) to the engine block and tighten to 25 N·m (18 lb ft).
  4. Secure the battery positive and negative cable to the engine with fasteners (2).


  5. Install the battery positive cable (3) and nut (2) to the engine fuse block and tighten to 10 N·m (89 lb in).
  6. Install the battery positive cable clamp to the battery post and tighten the nut (1) to 10 N·m (89 lb in).


  7. Install the negative battery cable (2) and nut (1) to the fuse block and tighten to 12 N·m (106 lb in).
  8. Connect the negative battery cable to the battery. Refer to Battery Negative Cable Disconnection and Connection


  9. Install the body harness terminal (2) to the negative battery stud and tighten nut (1) to10 N·m (89 lb in).
  10. Install the fuse block cover.
  11. Reset the radio channel presets.
