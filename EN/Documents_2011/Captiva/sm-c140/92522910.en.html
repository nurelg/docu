Captiva
   

Exhaust Manifold Installation — LE5

    Caution: Refer to Fastener Caution in the Preface section.



  1. Install new exhaust manifold studs (1) and tighten to 10 N·m (89 lb in).


  2. Install the secondary air injection pipe assembly (1).
  3. Install the secondary air injection pipe assembly bolts (2) and tighten to 10 N·m(89 lb in).


  4. Install the exhaust manifold gasket.
  5. Install the exhaust manifold (1) to the cylinder head.
  6. Install NEW exhaust manifold to cylinder head retaining nuts finger tight.


  7. Tighten the NEW exhaust manifold to cylinder head retaining nuts two passes in sequence.
    8. 7.1. Tighten first pass in sequence to 14 N·m(124 lb in).
    7.2. Tighten final pass in sequence to 14 N·m(124 lb in).


  8. Install the block heater (1) and tighten the bolt to 10 N·m (89 lb in).


  9. Install the exhaust manifold heat shield (1).
  10. Install the exhaust manifold heat shield bolts and tighten to 25 N·m (18 lb ft).


  11. Coat the threads of the oxygen sensor (1) with anti-seize. Refer to Adhesives, Fluids, Lubricants, and Sealers .
  12. Install the oxygen sensor and tighten to 42 N·m (31 lb ft).


  13. Install the secondary air injection studs (3 and 4) and tighten to 10 N·m(89 lb in).
  14. Install the secondary air injection valve assembly (1) with a NEW gasket (2).


  15. Install the secondary air injection bolts (2, 3) and nuts (1).
    16. 15.1. Tighten the secondary air injection assembly to cylinder head bolts (3) to 22 N·m(16 lb ft)
    15.2. Tighten the secondary air injection assembly to pipe assembly bolts (2) to 10 N·m(89 lb in)
    15.3. Tighten the secondary air injection assembly nuts (1) to 22 N·m(16 lb ft)
