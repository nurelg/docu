DTC Descriptor

DTC B1240 00: CD Mechanism Malfunction

Circuit/System Description

The radio monitors the CD mechanism for any errors or malfunctions during operation.

Conditions for Running the DTC

This test runs continuously.

Conditions for Setting the DTC

A malfunction is detected in the CD mechanism.

Action Taken When the DTC Sets

CD operation will be disabled.

Conditions for Clearing the DTC

The DTC will clear when a CD mechanism malfunction is no longer detected.
