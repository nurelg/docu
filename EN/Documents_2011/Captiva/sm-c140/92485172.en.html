Captiva
   

Water Pump Replacement - LNQ

Special Tools

BO 38185 Hose Clamp Pliers

For equivalent tools, refer toSpecial Tools .

Removal Procedure
  1. Drain the cooling system. Refer to Cooling System Draining and Filling .
  2. Remove the air conditioner compressor. Refer to Air Conditioning Compressor Replacement : Diesel  → V6  → 2.4L .
  3. Remove the drive belt. Refer to Drive Belt Replacement .
  4. Remove the diesel particulate filter. Refer to Exhaust Particulate Filter Replacement : LNQ .
  5. Remove the radiator outlet hose. Refer to Radiator Outlet Hose Replacement : Diesel  → V6  → LE5 .


  6. Disconnect the following from the water pump:
    7. • Thermostat bypass hose (1)
    • Engine coolant inlet hose (2)
    • Water pump inlet hose (3)


  7. Remove the water pump bolts (1) and discard the seal (2).
  8. Remove the water pump (3) from the engine compartment.

Installation Procedure

    Caution: Refer to Fastener Caution in the Preface section.



  1. Install the water pump (3) with a NEW seal (2) and tighten the bolts (1) to 25 N·m (18 lb ft).


  2. Connect the following to the water pump:
    3. • Thermostat bypass hose (1)
    • Engine coolant inlet hose (2)
    • Water pump inlet hose (3)
  3. Install the radiator outlet hose. Refer to Radiator Outlet Hose Replacement : Diesel  → V6  → LE5 .
  4. Install the air conditioner compressor. Refer to Air Conditioning Compressor Replacement : Diesel  → V6  → 2.4L .
  5. Install the drive belt. Refer to Drive Belt Replacement .
  6. Install the diesel particulate filter. Refer to Exhaust Particulate Filter Replacement : LNQ .
  7. Fill the cooling system. Refer to Cooling System Draining and Filling .
