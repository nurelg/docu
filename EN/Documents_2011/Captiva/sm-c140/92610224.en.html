Rear Seat Replacement - with AM9

Removal Procedure


  1. Remove the rear seat riser finish covers.
  2. Remove the 4 rear seat bolts (1).


  3. Pull the rear seat back cushion latch release located on the top of the rear seat back cushion, and fold the rear seat back cushion (2) forward.


  4. Unlatch the rear seat (2) by pushing upward on the bottom of the rear seat assembly.
  5. Remove the rear seat from the vehicle.

Installation Procedure


  1. Install the rear seat (2) to the vehicle, locating the front seat riser pins into the floor.
  2. Secure the rear of the seat by pushing down on the back of the seat ensuring that the seat is securely latched.


  3. Lift the rear seat back cushion into the full up right seat position.

    4. Caution: Refer to Fastener Caution in the Preface section.



  4. Install the 4 seat attachments bolts (1) to the front of the seat risers.

    5. Tighten
    Tighten the bolts to 40N·m(30 lb ft).
