Captiva
   

Transmission Control Replacement

Removal Procedure

    Warning: Refer to Battery Disconnect Warning in the Preface section.



  1. Disconnect the negative battery cable.
  2. Remove the console. Refer to Front Floor Console Replacement : Short Body → Long Body .
  3. Disconnect the shift control cables from the shift control lever housing.
  4. Remove the nuts from the shift control lever housing.
  5. Remove the shift control lever housing.


  6. Remove the shift control cable adjust locks (1).
  7. Remove the shift control cables (2) from the shift control lever housing bracket.
  8. Disconnect the shift control cables from the shift control adjusters.


  9. Remove the shift control cables (1) from the transaxle using a suitable tool.
  10. Remove the cables (2) from the shift control cable bracket.


  11. Remove the grommet from the dash panel.
  12. Remove the shift control cables from the vehicle.

Installation Procedure


  1. Install the shift control lever housing.

    2. Caution: Refer to Fastener Caution in the Preface section.

  2. Install the nuts to the shift control lever housing and tighten to 8 N·m (71 lb in).
  3. Connect the shift cables to the shift control lever housing, and adjust the cable.


  4. Install the shift control cables to the vehicle.

    5. Note: Ensure that the shift control cable pass-through grommet is properly seated and the grommet arrow is pointing up.

  5. Install the grommet from the dash panel.


  6. Install the cables (2) to the shift control cable bracket.
  7. Install the shift control cables (1) to the transaxle.
  8. Position the transaxle into Neutral.


  9. Install the shift control cables to the shift control lever housing bracket.


  10. Position the gear lever into Neutral.
  11. With the shift cable ends in the adjusters, adjust the cables by pushing the neutral lock pin. Move the shifter slightly to centre the neutral lock pin.


  12. Press and lock the shift control cable adjust locks.
  13. Pull the neutral lock pin to the original position.
  14. Install the console. Refer to Front Floor Console Replacement : Short Body → Long Body .
  15. Connect the negative battery cable.
