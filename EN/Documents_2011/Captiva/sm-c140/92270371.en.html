Captiva
   

Inlet Manifold Replacement - LD9

Removal Procedure

    Warning: Refer to Battery Disconnect Warning in the Preface section.



  1. Disconnect the negative battery cable.
  2. Drain the engine coolant. Refer to Cooling System Draining and Filling .
  3. Disconnect the intake air temperature (IAT) sensor connector.
  4. Loosen the clamp and disconnect the air cleaner outlet hose from the electronic throttle body with resonator attached.


  5. Disconnect the electronic throttle control (ETC) sensor connector.
  6. Disconnect the manifold absolute pressure (MAP) sensor connector.
  7. Disconnect the coolant hoses from the electronic throttle body.
  8. Disconnect all of the necessary vacuum hoses, including the brake servo vacuum hose at the inlet manifold.


  9. Remove the alternator upper bracket.
  10. Remove the alternator-to-inlet manifold strap bracket bolt on the inlet manifold and loosen the bolt on the alternator.


  11. Remove the fuel rail assembly. Refer to Fuel Injection Fuel Rail Assembly Replacement : LE5 or LE9 → LD9 .


  12. Remove the evaporative (EVAP) emission canister purge solenoid valve. Refer to Evaporative Emission Canister Purge Solenoid Valve Replacement : LE5 or LE9 → LD9 .


  13. Remove the inlet manifold support bracket bolt at the engine block and the inlet manifold.
  14. Remove the inlet manifold support bracket.
  15. Remove the ground terminal retaining bolt.


  16. Remove the inlet manifold retaining bolt and nuts in the sequence shown.
  17. Remove the intake manifold.
  18. Remove the inlet manifold gasket .
  19. Clean the sealing surfaces of the inlet manifold and the cylinder head.

Installation Procedure


  1. Install a new inlet manifold gasket.
  2. Install the inlet manifold.

    3. Caution: Refer to Fastener Caution in the Preface section.

  3. Install the inlet manifold retaining bolt and nuts in the sequence shown and tighten to 22 N·m (16 lb ft).


  4. Install the ground wiring terminal retaining bolt to the inlet manifold.
  5. Install the inlet manifold support bracket and tighten bolts and cylinder block to 25 N·m (18 lb ft).


  6. Install the alternator upper bracket and tighten:
    7. • retaining bolts (1) to 25 N·m (18 lb ft)
    • bolt (2) to 35 N·m (26 lb ft)
  7. Install the alternator-to-inlet manifold strap bracket and tighten:
    8. • retaining bolt (3) to 35 N·m (26 lb ft)
    •  bolt (4) to 25 N·m (18 lb ft)


  8. Install the fuel rail assembly and tighten bolts to 25 N·m (18 lb ft). Refer to Fuel Injection Fuel Rail Assembly Replacement : LE5 or LE9 → LD9 .


  9. Install the EVAP emission canister purge solenoid valve and tighten bolt to 5 N·m (44 lb in).


  10. Connect all of the necessary vacuum lines those were previously disconnected.
  11. Connect the MAP sensor connector.
  12. Connect the ETC sensor connector.
  13. Connect the coolant hoses to the electronic throttle body.


  14. Connect the air cleaner outlet hose to the electronic throttle body with resonator attached.
  15. Connect the IAT sensor connector.
  16. Connect the negative battery cable.
  17. Refill the engine cooling system. Refer to Cooling System Draining and Filling .
