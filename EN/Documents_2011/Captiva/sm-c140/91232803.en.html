Transmission Fluid Pump Disassemble

Tools Required

J 45000 Seal Remover



  1. Remove 13 transmission fluid pump cover bolts (231) and 1 transmission fluid pump cover bolt (232) to separate the fluid pump body from the fluid pump stator shaft assembly (208).


  2. Remove the fluid pump plate (207) from the fluid pump body (202).
  3. Remove the fluid pump O-ring seal (206) from the fluid pump body (202).


  4. Push the fluid pump driven gear (204) to one side of the fluid pump body (202). Using a feeler gauge, measure the clearance between the fluid pump driven gear (204) and the fluid pump body (202).

    5. Specification
    Driven gear to body clearance: 0.075 - 0.20 mm (0.002 - 0.007 in)



  5. Using a feeler gauge, measure between the fluid pump drive gear (205) teeth and the crescent shaped part of the fluid pump body (202).

    6. Specification
    Drive gear tip to body clearance: 0.013 - 0.342 mm (0.0005 - 0.014 in)



  6. Using a feeler gauge, measure between the fluid pump driven gear (204) teeth and the crescent shaped part of the fluid pump body (202).

    7. Specification
    Driven gear tip to body clearance: 0.004 - 0.298 mm (0.0001 - 0.618 in)



  7. Using a steel straight edge and a feeler gauge, measure the side clearance of both fluid pump gears in relationship to the fluid pump body.

    8. Specification
    Side clearance of drive and driven gear to body: 0.03 - 0.10 mm (0.0012 - 0.004 in)

  8. If any of the above measurements are not within specifications, replace the fluid pump assembly.


  9. Remove the fluid pump drive gear (205) and the fluid pump driven gear (204).


    10. Important: Do not damage the fluid pump body bush.

  10. Using J 45000, remove the fluid pump seal assembly (200) from the fluid pump body (202).
