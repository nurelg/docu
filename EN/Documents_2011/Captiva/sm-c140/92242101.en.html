Rear Seat Back Cushion Panel Replacement



Callout

Component Name

Preliminary Procedures

  1. Remove the rear seat. Refer to Rear Seat Replacement : with AM9  → without AM9 .
  2. Remove the rear seat recliner covers. Refer to Rear Seat Recliner Outer Finish Cover Replacement - Inboard : With 60 Percent Seat  → With 40 Percent Seat and Rear Seat Recliner Outer Finish Cover Replacement - Outboard : With 60 Percent Seat  → With 40 Percent Seat .
  3. Remove the rear seat head restraint. Refer to Rear Seat Head Restraint Replacement : AQP  → AM9 .
  4. Remove the seat belt opening cover, if equipped. Refer to Rear Seat Shoulder Belt Opening Bezel Replacement .
  5. Remove the rear child seat bracket trim cover. Refer to Child Seat Bracket Access Hole Cover Replacement .
  6. Remove the rear seat latch release strap bezel. Refer to Rear Seat Latch Release Strap Bezel Replacement .

1

Rear Seat Back Cushion Panel

Procedure

  1. Disconnect the J-channel retainer on the bottom and reposition the cover and pad to access the back panel.
  2. Slide each of the clips on the back panel to the enlarged portion of each slot.
  3. Pull rearward on the back panel to remove.
  4. Position the clips to the back panel and clip onto the back frame wires for installation.
