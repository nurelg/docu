Brake Warning Indicator Malfunction

Diagnostic Aids

The BRAKE lamp glows brightly when the ignition is ON and either the handbrake lever switch or the brake fluid level switch is closed. If neither switch is closed, the BRAKE lamp receives a ground through the alternator, and it glows dimly when the ignition is ON and the engine is off. When the engine starts, the alternator creates voltage. With voltage on both sides of the lamp, the lamp turns off.
