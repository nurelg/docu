Captiva
   

Rear Toe Adjustment

  1. Measure the alignment on an alignment rack. Refer to Wheel Alignment Specifications .


  2. Remove and discard the toe link to frame bolt, nut and the washer.

    3. Note: DO NOT completely tighten the NEW bolt and nut, just finger tighten.

  3. Install the NEW cam washer and bolt to the toe link.

    4. Note: It maybe necessary to re-inspect the toe setting.

  4. Rotate the cam bolt to the specified setting. Refer to Wheel Alignment Specifications .
  5. Hold the adjuster bolt and tighten the nut to 100 N·m (74 lb ft)
  6. Lower the vehicle.
