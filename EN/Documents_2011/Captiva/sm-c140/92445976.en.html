Power Brake Booster Vacuum Check Valve and Hose Replacement — LHD 2.4L

Removal Procedure
  1. With the engine OFF, apply and release the brake pedal several times until the brake pedal becomes firm to deplete the power vacuum brake booster vacuum reserve.


  2. Remove the power brake booster vacuum check valve (1) from the power vacuum brake booster.


  3. Release the spring clamp and remove the power brake booster vacuum check valve and hose assembly (1) from the intake manifold vacuum port.

Installation Procedure


  1. Install the power brake booster vacuum check valve and hose assembly (1) to the intake manifold vacuum port and secure the spring clamp.


    2. Note: A small amount of methylated spirit may be used to ease assembly. Do not use soap.

  2. Install the power brake booster vacuum check valve (1) to the power vacuum brake booster.
