Transmission Fluid Pump Cleaning and Inspection

Tools Required

J 45185 Bore Gauge Set



  1. Using the J 45185, measure the inner diameter of the fluid pump body bush. Measure the bush in different locations and take the average.
  2. Use micrometre to measure J 45185 reading.

    3. Specification
    Fluid pump body bush I.D: 38.113 - 38.188 mm (1.500 - 1.503 in)



  3. Using the J 45185, measure the inner diameter of the stator shaft bush. Measure the bush in different locations and take the average.
  4. Use micrometre to measure J 45185 reading.

    5. Specification
    Stator shaft bush I.D: 22.201 - 22.257 mm (0.874 - 0.876 in)

  5. Replace the fluid pump assembly if the measurements are greater than the specification.
