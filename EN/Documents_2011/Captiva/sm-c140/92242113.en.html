Driver or Passenger Seat Back Cushion Heater Replacement



Callout

Component Name

Preliminary Procedures

  1. Remove the front seat. Refer to Front Seat Replacement - Bucket .
  2. Remove the front seat back panel. Refer to Driver or Passenger Seat Back Cushion Finish Panel Replacement .
  3. Remove the front seat head restraint. Refer to Driver or Passenger Seat Head Restraint Replacement .
  4. Remove the front seat head restraint guides. Refer to Driver or Passenger Seat Head Restraint Guide Replacement .
  5. Remove the front seat lumbar support actuator knob and bezel, if equipped. Refer to Driver Seat Lumbar Support Actuator Knob Bezel Replacement .
  6. Remove the front seat outer adjuster finish cover. Refer to Driver or Passenger Seat Outer Adjuster Finish Cover Replacement : Without Power Seat  → With Power Seat .
  7. Remove the front seat inner adjuster finish cover. Refer to Driver or Passenger Seat Inner Adjuster Finish Cover Replacement .

1

Front Seat Back Cushion Cover

Procedure

  1. Disengage the J-channel retainers from the seat back cushion frame.
  2. Disconnect the electrical connectors that attach to the seat back cushion, if equipped.
  3. Remove the inflatable restraint module C-rings from the seat cover, use new C-rings when installing.
  4. Remove the seat back cushion cover and pad as an assembly.
  5. Pull the cover away from the pad to disengage the retainers.
