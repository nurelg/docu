2nd Clutch Disassemble

Special Tools

J-28585 Circlip Remover



  1. Using J-28585 remover remove the second clutch backing plate retaining ring (220) from the fluid pump assembly.


  2. Remove the second clutch backing plate (219).
  3. Remove the 4 fibre plates (217).
  4. Remove the 4 steel plates (218).


  5. Remove the second clutch spring assembly (215).


  6. Remove the second clutch spring assembly retaining ring (214).


    7. Warning: Wear safety glasses when using compressed air in order to prevent eye injury.

  7. While pulling on the piston by hand, apply compressed air into the fluid passage and remove the second clutch piston (210) and the second clutch piston sleeve (212).
  8. Remove the second clutch piston outer seal (209) from the second clutch piston (210).
  9. Remove the second clutch piston sleeve seals (211, 213) from the second clutch piston sleeve (212).
