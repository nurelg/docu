DTC Descriptor

DTC B1000 00 : Electronic Control Unit Performance

Circuit/System Description

When the ignition switch is turned to ON, the inflatable restraint sensing and diagnostic module (SDM) will perform turn-on test to diagnose critical malfunctions within SDM itself.

Conditions for Running the DTC

Ignition voltage is between 9-16 volts.

Conditions for Setting the DTC

The SDM does not implement diagnostic operation. In case of above, the wiring harness of SDM should be checked before replacing the SDM.

Action Taken When the DTC Sets
    • The SDM commands ON the AIR BAG warning lamp via serial data communications.
    • The SDM disables all deployments.

Conditions for Clearing the DTC

The condition responsible for setting the DTC no longer exists and the scan tool Clear DTCs function is used.

Diagnostic Aids

This DTC is an indication that an incorrect SDM is installed in the vehicle, or that the SDM and/or the BCM was replaced without reprogramming the BCM with the new information.

    2. ⇒If DTC B1000 was current replace the SDM.
  2. Ignition ON, use the scan tool to clear the DTCs.
