Engine Front Cover Replacement

Special Tools

EN-50435 Guide Pin

For equivalent regional tools, refer to Special Tools .

Removal Procedure
  1. Install the engine support fixture. Refer to Engine Support Fixture .
  2. Remove the drive belt and drive belt tensioner. Refer to Drive Belt Tensioner Replacement .
  3. Remove the crankshaft pulley and cover. Refer to Crankshaft Pulley Replacement .
  4. Remove the engine mount and bracket. Refer to Engine Mount Bracket Replacement .


  5. Remove the engine front cover to sump bolts (1).


  6. Remove the engine front cover bolts.
  7. Remove the engine front cover (2).
  8. If necessary, remove the front crankshaft seal. Refer to Crankshaft Front Oil Seal Replacement .

Installation Procedure
  1. If removed, install a new front crankshaft seal. Refer to Crankshaft Front Oil Seal Replacement .


    2. Note: Ensure that the front cover surface is clear of oil, grease and old sealant material.

  2. Apply sealant (Three-Bond, 12378521) on the sealant line (1).
  3. Also apply sealant (Three-Bond, 12378521) on bottom of the front cover at the sump sealing surface.

    4. Note: The alignment pins will help maintain position while installing the bolts.

  4. To aid in cover alignment, install two EN-50435 Guide Pins on the engine block.
  5. Install the engine front cover.


  6. Start front cover bolts (1) and remove EN-50435 Guide Pins guide pins.

    7. Caution: Refer to Fastener Caution in the Preface section.



  7. Install the remainder of the engine front cover bolts and tighten to the following:
    8. • Timing Chain Front Cover Retaining Bolt (M8): 22 N·m (16 lb ft)
    • Timing Chain Front Cover Retaining Bolt (M6): 9 N·m (80 lb in)


  8. Install the engine front cover to sump bolts and tighten to 25 N·m (18 lb ft).
  9. Install the engine mount and bracket. Refer to Engine Mount Bracket Replacement .
  10. Install the crankshaft pulley and cover. Refer to Crankshaft Pulley Replacement .
  11. Install the drive belt tensioner and drive belt. Refer to Drive Belt Tensioner Replacement .
  12. Remove the engine support fixture.
