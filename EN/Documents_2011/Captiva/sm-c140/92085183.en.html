Transmission Replacement

Removal Procedure


  1. Remove the battery and the battery tray. Refer to Battery Tray Replacement .
  2. Remove the power steering oil reservoir mounting bolts and put the reservoir aside.
  3. Remove the pin and the clutch actuator cylinder pipe.


  4. Remove the upper transaxle-to-engine retaining bolts.


  5. Install the engine support fixture.
  6. Raise and suitably support the vehicle. Refer to Lifting and Jacking the Vehicle .


  7. Remove the engine under cover.
  8. Remove the left hand side dust cover.


  9. Remove the front exhaust pipe. Refer to Front Pipe Replacement : LNQ → LF1 → LE5 .


  10. Carefully remove the frame from the vehicle. Refer to Frame Replacement .
  11. Remove the drive axle shafts.


  12. Remove the shift control cables (1) from the transaxle.
  13. Remove the cables (2) from the shift control cable bracket.


  14. Disconnect the reversing lamp switch electrical wiring connector.


  15. Remove the front damper bush mounting bolts and the front damper bush.


  16. Remove the rear transaxle mounting bracket bolts.
  17. Remove the rear damper bush and the rear transaxle mounting bracket.


  18. For AWD, remove the transfer case from the transaxle.


  19. Remove the starter. Refer to Starter Replacement : LNQ → LF1 → LD9 → LE5 .


  20. Support the transaxle assembly using the supporting jack.


  21. Remove the oil pan flange-to-transaxle bolts (1).
  22. Remove the right lower engine-to-transaxle bolts (2) near the front damper bush.


  23. Remove the left upper transaxle mounting bracket bolts.


  24. Carefully remove the transaxle assembly from the vehicle.

Installation Procedure


  1. Install the transaxle into the vehicle and support the transaxle assembly using the supporting jack.

    2. Caution: Refer to Fastener Caution in the Preface section.



  2. Install the four left upper transaxle mounting bracket bolts and tighten to 50 N·m (37 lb ft).


  3. Install the right lower engine-to-transaxle bolts near the front damper bush and tighten to 75 N·m (55 lb ft).
  4. Remove the transaxle support jack.
  5. Install the oil pan flange-to-transaxle bolts (1) and tighten to 50 N·m (37 lb ft).


  6. Install the starter. Refer to Starter Replacement : LNQ → LF1 → LD9 → LE5 .


  7. For AWD, install the transfer case into the transaxle.


  8. Install the rear damper bush and the rear transaxle mounting bracket.
  9. Install the rear transaxle mounting bracket bolts and tighten to 90 N·m (66 lb ft).


  10. Install the front damper bush and the front damper bush mounting bolts and tighten to 50 N·m (37 lb ft).
  11. Install the drive axle shafts.


  12. Connect the reversing lamp switch electrical wiring connector.


  13. Install the cables (2) to the shift control cable bracket.
  14. Install the shift control cables (1) to the transaxle.


  15. Carefully install the frame into the vehicle. Refer to Frame Replacement .


  16. Install the front exhaust pipe. Refer to Front Pipe Replacement : LNQ → LF1 → LE5 .


  17. Install the left hand side dust cover.
  18. Install the engine under cover.
  19. Lower the vehicle.
  20. Remove the engine support fixture.


  21. Install the upper transaxle-to-engine bolts and tighten.
    22. • For 2.0L or 2.4L engines, tighten the upper transaxle-to-engine bolts to 75 N·m (55 lb ft).
    • For 2.8L, 3.2L, or 3.6L engines, tighten the upper transaxle-to-engine bolts to50 N·m (37 lb ft).


  22. Install the pin and the clutch actuator cylinder pipe.
  23. Install the power steering oil reservoir mounting.
  24. Install the battery and the battery tray. Refer to Battery Tray Replacement .
  25. Bleed the air from the hydraulic clutch. Refer to Hydraulic Clutch System Bleeding .
  26. Adjust the shift control cable.
  27. Check the fluid level.
