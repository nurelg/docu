Procedure

  1. Take extreme care to prevent any scratches or damage to the camshafts.
  2. Mark the camshafts upon removal to ensure installation is in the correct position.
  3. Lubricate the camshaft and camshaft bearing caps with engine oil.
  4. Transfer parts as necessary.
