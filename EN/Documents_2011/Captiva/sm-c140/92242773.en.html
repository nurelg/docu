Roof Panel Joint Finish Moulding Replacement - Short Body



Callout

Component Name

1

Roof Panel Exterior Moulding

Procedure

  1. Remove the roof panel exterior moulding starting at the rear of the vehicle.
  2. Release the six moulding retainers from the body panel.
  3. Upon installation, position the front end of the moulding over the forward body panel stud.
  4. Position the moulding by aligning the other 6 retainers to the body panel studs.
  5. Secure the moulding working from the front edge to the rear by pressing firmly the moulding retainers onto the body panel studs.
