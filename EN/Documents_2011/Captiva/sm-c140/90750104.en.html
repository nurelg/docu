3rd Gear Band Servo Piston Assembly Removal



  1. Rotate the transmission assembly so the rear case cover assembly is facing up.
  2. Remove the third gear servo cover retaining ring (60) from the transmission case assembly (17).


  3. Remove the third gear band servo cover (59) from the transmission case assembly.
  4. Remove the 2 O-ring seals (58) from the third gear band servo cover (59).


  5. Remove the third gear band servo piston assembly (57).
  6. Remove the third gear band servo compression spring (56).
