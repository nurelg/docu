Captiva
   

2nd Coast Clutch Plate Disassemble

Special Tools

    • J-23327 Clutch Spring Compressor
    • J-28585 Circlip Remover
    • J-41236 Coast Clutch Spring Compressor
    • J-45164 Spring Compressor Bridge


  1. Using J-45164 bridge  (2), J 21420-2 (4) and J 23327-2 (1), which are part of J-23327 compressor, compress the second clutch spring.
  2. Using J-28585 remover  (3), remove the second coast clutch backing plate retaining ring (230) from the fluid pump assembly.


  3. Remove the clutch plates (227, 228, 229).


    4. Note: Do not let the tools contact the bush surfaces.

  4. Install J 21420-2 (1) and J 23327-2 (3), which are part of J-23327 compressor, and J-41236 compressor  (2) and compress the second coast clutch spring (224).
  5. Remove the second coast clutch spring retaining ring (225) using circlip pliers.
  6. Remove the second coast clutch spring (224) from the fluid pump assembly.


    7. Warning: Wear safety glasses when using compressed air in order to prevent eye injury.

  7. While pulling upward on the piston by hand, apply compressed air into the fluid passage in the fluid pump assembly and remove the second coast clutch piston (222).
  8. Remove the inner seal (221) and the outer seal (223) from the second coast clutch piston (222).
