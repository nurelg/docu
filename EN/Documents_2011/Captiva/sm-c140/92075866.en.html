Park Brake Hardware Inspection

Warning: Refer to Brake Dust Warning in the Preface section.

  1. With the brake rotors removed, visually inspect the upper return/adjuster actuator spring and the lower return spring for the following:
    2. • Excessive corrosion
    • Excessive stretching
    • Broken hook ends
    • Twisting or binding
  2. If any of the conditions listed were found, which could lead to improper park brake apply and/or release, the adjuster hardware and/or return springs require replacement.
  3. Visually inspect the adjuster and the adjuster actuator lever for the following:
    4. • Misalignment with the park brake lever
    • Misalignment with the park brake shoes
    • Broken or bent adjuster actuator lever
    • Bent adjuster actuator lever pivot pin
  4. If any of the conditions listed were found, which could lead to improper park brake apply and/or release, the adjuster hardware requires repair or replacement.
  5. Inspect the park brake lever for the following:
    6. • Bent
    • Broken pivot pin
    • Seized at the pivot pin
    • Broken at park brake cable attachment
    • Improper contact with the adjuster
  6. If any of the conditions listed were found, which could lead to improper park brake apply and/or release, the park brake lever requires repair or replacement.
  7. Visually inspect the park brake cable for the following:
    8. • Improper mounting to the backing plate
    • Improper mounting at the park brake lever
    • Broken or missing cable end lug
  8. If any of the conditions listed were found, which could lead to improper park brake apply and/or release, the park brake cable requires repair or replacement.
  9. Apply the park brake lever/pedal three clicks.
  10. Visually inspect the park brake cable for the following:
    11. • Unequal travel between the left and the right park brake systems
    • No travel on one or both of the park brake systems
  11. If any of the conditions listed were found, which could lead to improper park brake apply and/or release, the park brake cable requires replacement and/or adjustment.
  12. Release the park brake lever/pedal.
  13. Visually inspect the park brake cable for the following:
    14. • Unequal release between the left and the right park brake systems
    • No release at one or both of the park brake systems
  14. If any of the conditions listed were found, which could lead to improper park brake apply and/or release, the park brake cable requires replacement and/or adjustment.
