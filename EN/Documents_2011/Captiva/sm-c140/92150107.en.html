Bonnet Adjustment



  1. Loosen the hinge-to-body bolts to adjust the bonnet fore and aft, and cross-vehicle. Position the bonnet as needed.


  2. Adjust the bonnet latch and bonnet bumpers to adjust the bonnet up and down at the front of the bonnet.

    3. Caution : Refer to Fastener Caution in the Preface section.

  3. Tighten the hinge and latch bolts.

    4. Tighten

        • Tighten the hinge-to-bonnet bolts to 25 N·m (18 lb ft).
        • Tighten the hinge-to-body bolts to 25 N·m (18 lb ft).
        • Tighten the bonnet latch fastener to 25 N·m (18 lb ft).
