Crankshaft Pulley Replacement



Callout

Component Name

Preliminary Procedures

  1. Remove the drive belt. Refer to Drive Belt Replacement .
  2. Remove the engine mount. Refer to Engine Mount Replacement - Right Side .
  3. Remove the starter. Refer to Starter Replacement : LNQ  → LF1  → LE5 .

1

Crankshaft Pulley Bolt

Caution: Refer to Fastener Caution in the Preface section.

Procedure

  1. Install EN-50432 Flywheel Fixing Adapter through the starter opening and ensure it is fully engaged into the flexplate.
  2. Using EN-50435 Crankshaft Pulley Bolt Remover , remove the crankshaft pulley bolt.

Tighten

    • 120 N·m (89 lb ft)
    • Tighten the bolt an additional 90 degrees

Special Tools

    • EN-50432 Flywheel Fixing Adaptor
    • EN-50435 Crankshaft Pulley Bolt Remover

For equivalent regional tools, refer to Special Tools .
