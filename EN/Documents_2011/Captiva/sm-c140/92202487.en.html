Camshaft Removal - Left Side



  1. Observe the markings on the bearing caps. Each bearing cap is marked in order to identify its location. The markings have the following meanings:
    2. • The raised feature must always be oriented toward the center of the cylinder head.
    • The I indicates the intake camshaft.
    • The E indicates the exhaust camshaft.
    • The number indicates the journal position from the front of the engine.


  2. Remove the camshaft bearing cap bolts.
  3. Remove the camshaft bearing caps.


    4. Note: Mark the camshafts upon removal to ensure installation is in the correct position.

  4. Remove the camshafts.
  5. Replace the camshaft bearing caps and bolts.
