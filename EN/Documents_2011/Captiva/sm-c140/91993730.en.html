DTC B3810

Circuit Description

The body control module (BCM) provides a high side drive output through the headlamp washer relay control circuit that controls the headlamp washer relay.

DTC Descriptor

This diagnostic procedure supports the following DTC:

DTC B3810 Headlamp Washer Relay Circuit

This vehicle has DTCs which include DTC Symptoms.

DTC Symptom

DTC Symptom Descriptor

02

Short to Ground

05

Short to battery or open

Conditions for Running the DTC

The only requirements are valid operating voltage and ground.

Conditions for Setting the DTC

All of the following conditions exist for more than 1 second:

B3810 02

The BCM detects a short to ground in the headlamp washer relay control circuit when the output is active.

B3810 05

The BCM detects a short to battery voltage or an open in the headlamp washer relay control circuit when the output is inactive.

Action Taken When the DTC Sets

B3810 02

The BCM disables the headlamp washer function.

Conditions for Clearing the DTC
    • The current DTC clears when the malfunction is no longer present.
    • The history DTC clears when the module ignition cycle counter reaches the reset threshold, without a repeat of the malfunction.
