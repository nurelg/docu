Relay Replacement - Within an Electrical Center

Special Tools

J 43244 Relay Puller Pliers

Removal Procedure
  1. Remove the electrical center cover.
  2. Locate the relay. Refer to Electrical Centre Identification Views to locate the electrical centre where the relay exists.

    3. Note: 

       • Always note the orientation of the relay.
       • If equipped with a notch style relay; observe the location of the notch on the old relay to verify the new relay is installed with the notch in the same location.
       • Ensure that the electrical centre is secure, as not to put added stress on the wires or terminals.



  3. Using the J 43244 (1) position the tool on opposing corners of the relay (2).

    4. Caution: Use J43244 to pull the relay straight out from the electrical centre terminals. The use of pliers or a flat bladed tool could damage the electrical centre.

  4. Remove the relay (2) from the electrical centre.

Installation Procedure


  1. Install the relay (2) in the same position as removed.
  2. Install the electrical center cover.
