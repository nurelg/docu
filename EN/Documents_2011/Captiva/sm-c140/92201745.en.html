Oil Level Indicator Tube Replacement - LE5, LE9 or LAF

Removal Procedure


  1. Remove the air cleaner outlet duct. Refer to Air Cleaner Outlet Duct Replacement : LE5 or LE9 .
  2. Remove the oil level indicator.


  3. Remove the engine harness clip (2) from the oil level indicator tube bracket.


  4. Remove the oil level indicator tube bolt .
  5. Remove the oil level indicator tube.
  6. Remove the oil level indicator tube O-ring seals, if necessary.
  7. Disconnect electrical connector as needed.
  8. Disconnect the brake vacuum pump connectors.

Installation Procedure


  1. Install NEW oil level indicator tube O-ring seals, if necessary.
  2. Install the oil level indicator tube.

    3. Caution: Refer to Fastener Caution in the Preface section.

  3. Install the oil level indicator tube bolt and tighten to 10 N·m (89 lb in).


  4. Install the engine harness clip (1) to the oil level indicator tube bracket.


  5. Install the oil level indicator .
  6. Install the air cleaner outlet duct. Refer to Air Cleaner Outlet Duct Replacement : LE5 or LE9 .
  7. Connect electrical connector as needed.
  8. Reconnect the brake vacuum pump connectors.
