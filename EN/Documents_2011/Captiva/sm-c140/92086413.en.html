2

4-5-6 Clutch Piston Fluid Dam Assembly

Procedure

  1. Place the 3-5 reverse and 4-5-6 clutch housing onto the support hub in the case.
  2. Apply shop air to the 4-5-6 clutch feed hole using a rubber tipped air gun to dislodge the dam piston and the 4-5-6 clutch piston from the clutch housing.
