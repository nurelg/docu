For equivalent regional tools, refer to Special Tools .

Installation Procedure


  1. Remove the intake manifold cover. Refer to Intake Manifold Cover Replacement .
  2. Remove the air inlet screen. Refer to Air Inlet Grille Panel Replacement : Short Body .
  3. Install two M8 x 1.25 double-ended studs to each strut tower with 30 mm engagement to the strut tower and 16 mm engagement to the EN-28467-13 adapters .
  4. Install three EN-28467-13 adapters (1) and two J 28467-5 strut tower adapters (2) to the top of the left and right strut tower.
  5. Install a 127 cm (50 in) engine support fixture cross bar EN-28467-B support fixture (3) transversely across the vehicle between both J 28467-5 strut tower adapters (2).
  6. Insert safety pins J-28467-10 (4) through the J 28467-5 strut tower adapters (2) and the cross bar (3) to prevent movement.


  7. Install the EN-42451-1 engine support bracket to the engine.


  8. Install the EN-42451-1 engine support bracket to the engine.


  9. Position two 58 cm (23 in) engine support fixture cross bars (1) longitudinally with two EN-36462-A leg assemblies (2).
  10. Install two J 28467-1A clamps (3) to secure the longitudinal mounted cross bar to the transverse mounted cross bar.


  11. Assemble the l EN-28467-34 wing nut and the lift hook bracket J-28467-6A to the EN-28467-7A lift hook .


  12. Install the lift hook and bracket assembly (1) to the longitudinal mounted cross bar (2).


  13. Position the EN-28467-7A lift hook to the rear engine lift bracket EN-42451-1 engine support bracket (2).


  14. Assemble the EN-28467-34 wing nut and the lift hook bracket J-28467-6A to the EN-28467-7A lift hook .


  15. Install the lift hook and bracket assembly (1) to the longitudinal mounted cross bar (2).


  16. Position the EN-28467-7A bolt hook (1) to the front engine lift bracket EN-42451-1 engine support bracket (2).


  17. Evenly tighten both EN-28467-34 wing nuts (1) until the engine weight is supported by the engine support fixture and no longer carried by the engine mounts.
