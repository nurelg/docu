Power Brake Booster Vacuum Check Valve and Hose Replacement — LHD 3.0L

Removal Procedure
  1. With the engine OFF, apply and release the brake pedal several times until the brake pedal becomes firm to deplete the power vacuum brake booster vacuum reserve.
  2. Remove the intake manifold cover. Refer to Intake Manifold Cover Replacement .
  3. Disconnect the power brake booster vacuum sensor electrical connector.


  4. Release the spring clamp (1) and remove the power brake booster vacuum hose from the intake manifold vacuum port.
  5. Remove the power brake booster vacuum sensor (2) from the power vacuum brake booster.
  6. Disconnect the power brake booster vacuum hose quick connect (3) from the power brake booster pump.
  7. Remove the power brake booster vacuum hose and check valve assembly.

Installation Procedure


    Note: A small amount of methylated spirit may be used to ease assembly. Do not use soap.

  1. Release the tension on the spring clamp (1) and install the power brake booster vacuum hose to the intake manifold vacuum port.
  2. Install the power brake booster vacuum sensor (2) to the power vacuum brake booster.
  3. Connect the power brake booster vacuum hose quick connect (3) to the power brake booster pump.
  4. Connect the power brake booster vacuum sensor electrical connector.
  5. Install the inlet manifold cover. Refer to Intake Manifold Cover Replacement .
