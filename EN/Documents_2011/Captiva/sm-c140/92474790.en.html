Cylinder Head Disassemble

Special Tools

    • EN-46116 Stem Seal Installer
    • EN-46569 Valve Spring Compressor
    • EN-48247 Valve Spring Compression Adapter

For equivalent regional tools, refer to Special Tools .



  1. Install the EN-46569 valve spring compressor (1) with EN-48247 valve spring compression adapter (2) to remove the valve assembly.


  2. Remove the valve stem keys (1).
  3. Remove the valve spring retainers (2).
  4. Remove the valve springs (3).


  5. Remove the valve stem oil seals using the EN-46116 stem seal installer (1).


    6. Warning: Compressed valve springs have high tension against the valve spring compressor. Valve springs that are not properly compressed by or released from the valve spring compressor can be ejected from the valve spring compressor with intense force. Use care when compressing or releasing the valve spring with the valve spring compressor and when removing or installing the valve stem keys. Failing to use care may cause personal injury.

    Note: Take extreme care to prevent damage to the valve guides.

  6. Remove the 16 valves (5)
