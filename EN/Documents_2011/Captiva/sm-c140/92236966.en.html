3

Transfer Case Rear Output Shaft Housing

Caution: Do not use a drive hammer when the splines are not engaged. Use of a drive hammer when the splines are not engaged will damage the output shaft teeth.

Tip

    • Use of a heat gun allows easy alignment of the bolt holes to the housing during assembly.
    • Install the rear output shaft housing with the flat area aligned to the tab on the main housing.
    • Rotate the pinion gear during assembly to engage the gear teeth.
