Sunroof Housing Drain Gutter Replacement



Callout

Component Name

Preliminary Procedure

Remove the sunroof window. Refer to Sunroof Window Replacement .

1

Sunroof Water Gutter Hook Arm

Procedure

  1. Release the sunroof water gutter hook arms from the sunroof frame on both sides.
  2. Position the sunroof water gutter upright on both sides to location the tabs.

Tip
Mark the location of the hole on the panel which the water gutter hook arms hook into.

2

Sunroof Water Gutter

Procedure

Remove the sunroof water gutter tab from the sunroof guide insert a flat blade tool and push inward on the tab to release it.
