Forward and Direct Clutch Assembly Disassemble

Special Tools

    • J-23327 Clutch Spring Compressor
    • J-25024-A Servo Piston/Clutch Spring Compressor
    • J-28585 Circlip Remover
    • J-45186 Forward and Direct Clutch Spring Compressor


  1. Remove the input shaft O-ring seal (570) from the forward and direct clutch housing assembly - with input shaft (567).


  2. Remove the forward clutch housing thrust bearing (569) and the forward clutch housing thrust bearing race (568) from the forward and direct clutch housing assembly - with input shaft (567).


  3. Using J-28585 remover, remove the forward clutch backing plate retaining ring (550) from the forward and direct clutch housing assembly - with input shaft (567).


  4. Remove the following components from the forward and direct clutch housing assembly - with input shaft (567):
    5. • The forward clutch backing plate (551)
    • The 4 forward clutch fibre plates (552)
    • The 4 forward clutch steel plates (553)


  5. Using J-28585 remover, remove the direct clutch backing plate retaining ring (554) from the forward and direct clutch housing assembly - with input shaft (567).


  6. Remove the following components from the forward and direct clutch housing assembly - with input shaft (567):
    7. • The direct clutch backing plate (555)
    • The 4 direct clutch fibre plates (556)
    • The 4 direct clutch steel plates (557)
    • The direct clutch apply plate (558)


  7. Remove the direct clutch spring retaining ring (559).
    8. 7.1. Place the J-25024-A compressor  (1) in a vise with the forcing screw on the bottom and backed out.
    7.2. Place the J-45186 spring  (2) over the shaft of the forward direct clutch housing and input shaft assembly (567).
    7.3. Place the J-21420-2 (4) from the J-23327 compressor on top of the forcing screw of J-25024-A compressor  (1).
    7.4. With the forcing screw, compress the clutch spring to allow the removal of the retaining ring.
    7.5. Use J-28585 remover  (3) or circlip pliers to remove the retaining ring.
    7.6. Remove the tools.


  8. Remove the direct clutch spring assembly (560).


    9. Warning: Wear safety glasses when using compressed air in order to prevent eye injury.

  9. Install the forward and direct clutch housing assembly with input shaft (567) onto the transmission case cover (19).
  10. While pulling on the piston by hand, apply compressed air into the fluid passage (C2) in order to remove the direct clutch piston (562).


  11. Remove the direct clutch piston inner seal (561) and the direct clutch piston outer seal (563) from the direct clutch piston (562).


    12. Warning: Wear safety glasses when using compressed air in order to prevent eye injury.

  12. While pulling upward on the piston by hand, apply compressed air into the fluid passage (C1) in order to remove the forward clutch piston (565).


  13. Remove the forward clutch piston inner seal (564) and the forward clutch piston outer seal (566) from the forward clutch piston (565).
