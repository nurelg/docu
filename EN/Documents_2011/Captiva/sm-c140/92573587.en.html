Engine Block Disassemble - LE5 or LE9



  1. Remove the engine block coolant drain plug.
  2. Remove the rear oil passage plug.
  3. Remove the 6 oil passage plugs on the sides of the block .
  4. Remove the 2 water passage plugs on the top of the block .
  5. Remove the 2 front oil passage plugs.


  6. Remove the knock sensor and bolt.


  7. Remove the oil pressure switch .


  8. Remove the crankshaft position (CKP) sensor and bolt.


  9. If equipped, loosen the piston oil nozzle assembly bolt and remove the piston oil nozzle assembly.
