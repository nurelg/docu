Captiva
   

Water Pump Replacement - LF1

Special Tools

EN 46104 Water Pump Pulley Holder

For equivalent regional tools refer to Special Tools .

Removal Procedure
  1. Drain the cooling system. Refer to Cooling System Draining and Filling .
  2. Remove the generator drive belt. Refer to Drive Belt Replacement .


  3. Use the EN 46104 water pump pulley holder to retain the pulley.


  4. Remove the water pump pulley bolts .
  5. Remove the water pump pulley.


  6. Remove the water pump bolts.
  7. Remove the water pump.
  8. Remove and DISCARD the water pump seal.
  9. Carefully clean the water pump sealing surfaces.

Installation Procedure


  1. Install a NEW water pump seal.
  2. Install the water pump.

    3. Caution: Refer to Fastener Caution in the Preface section.

  3. Hand tighten the water pump bolts.


  4. Tighten the water pump bolts in sequence to 12 N·m (106 lb in).
  5. Tighten the water pump bolts a second pass in sequence to 12 N·m (106 lb in).


  6. Install the water pump pulley and the water pump pulley bolts.


  7. Use the EN 46104 water pump pulley holder to hold the pulley.
  8. Install the water pump pulley bolts and tighten to 12 N·m (108 lb in).
  9. Install the generator drive belt. Refer to Drive Belt Replacement .
  10. Fill the cooling system. Refer to Cooling System Draining and Filling .
