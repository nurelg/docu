Manual Shift Detent Lever and Park Pawl Removal



  1. Lift the manual shift detent spring (711) off of the manual shift detent lever assembly (710) to disengage the park pawl actuator rod (709) from the manual shift detent lever assembly (710).


  2. Remove the manual shift detent lever assembly (710) from the transmission case assembly.


  3. Remove the park pawl actuator rod (709) from the transmission case assembly.


  4. Remove the manual shift detent lever spring bolt/screw (712) and the park pawl actuator bracket bolt/screw (715).


  5. Remove the manual shift detent spring assembly (711) from the transmission case assembly.


  6. Remove the park pawl shaft (700).
  7. Remove the park pawl spring (701).


  8. Remove the park pawl actuator bracket (708) and park pawl (702).


  9. Remove the park pawl spring pin (703).


  10. Remove the park pawl bolt/screw (706).
  11. Remove the park pawl pin spring guide sleeve (705).
  12. Remove the park pawl pin spring (704).
