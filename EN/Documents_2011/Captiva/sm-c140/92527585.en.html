Camshaft Bearing Cap Installation — Rear

  1. Clean the sealing surfaces.


    2. Note: The complete installation procedure of the camshaft guide should be done in 10 minutes.

    Note: Do not coat sealing compound on the camshaft guide bolt holes.

  2. Apply sealing compound on the sealant line (1).


  3. Install the camshaft guide (1).

    4. Caution: Refer to Fastener Caution in the Preface section.

  4. Install the 3 camshaft guide retaining bolts (3) and tighten to 25 N·m (18 lb ft).
  5. Install the 2 camshaft guide retaining bolts (2) and tighten to 10 N·m (89 lb in).
