Control Valve Body Cover Installation



  1. Clean any gasket material from the control valve body cover and inspect the gasket mounting surface.
  2. Apply a 3 mm (1/8 in) bead of sealant, to the cover mounting surface. Refer to Adhesives, Fluids, Lubricants, and Sealers .


  3. Install the control valve body cover (29) onto the transmission case assembly.
  4. Apply threadlocker, to the threads of all of the cover bolts. Refer to Adhesives, Fluids, Lubricants, and Sealers .
  5. Install the 9 - M8 x 1.25 x 15.5 mm control valve body cover bolts (28).


    6. Caution: Use the correct fastener in the correct location. Replacement fasteners must be the correct part number for that application. Fasteners requiring replacement or fasteners requiring the use of thread locking compound or sealant are identified in the service procedure. Do not use paints, lubricants, or corrosion inhibitors on fasteners or fastener joint surfaces unless specified. These coatings affect fastener torque and joint clamping force and may damage the fastener. Use the correct tightening sequence and specifications when installing fasteners in order to avoid damage to parts and systems.

  6. Tighten the 9 control valve body cover bolts in sequence. Tighten the control valve body cover bolts to 13 N·m (10 lb ft).
