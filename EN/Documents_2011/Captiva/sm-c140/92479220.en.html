Engine Front Cover Installation

Special Tools

EN-50435 Guide Pin

For equivalent regional tools, refer to Special Tools .

  1. Remove any old thread sealant, gasket material or sealant using remover such as chisel or scraper.
  2. Clean the front cover and front cover components with solvent.

    3. Note: Ensure that the sealing surface is clear of oil, grease and old sealant material.

  3. Clean out debris from the bolt holes.

    4. Warning: Refer to Safety Glasses Warning in the Preface section.

  4. Dry the front cover and front cover components with compressed air.


    5. Note: The complete installation procedure of the Front Cover should be done in 10 minutes.

    Note: Do not coat sealing compound on the Front Cover bolt holes and bolt threads.

  5. Apply sealing compound on sealant line (1), 2 hole faces (2) and 2 areas (3).
  6. Apply sealing compound to the edge of chamfer (4).


    7. Note: The alignment pins will help maintain position while installing the bolts.

  7. To aid in cover alignment, install two EN-50435 guide pins on the engine block.
  8. Install the engine front cover (1).
  9. Start engine front cover bolts and remove EN-50435 guide pins .

    10. Caution: Refer to Fastener Caution in the Preface section.

  10. Install the 13 engine front cover retaining bolts (M8) (3) and tighten the bolts evenly to 22 N·m (16 lb ft)
  11. Install 3 engine front cover retaining bolts (M6) (2) and tighten the bolts to 9 Nm (80 lb in).
