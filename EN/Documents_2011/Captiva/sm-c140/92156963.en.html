Captiva
   

Crankshaft Position Sensor Replacement - LD9

Removal Procedure

    Warning: Refer to Battery Disconnect Warning in the Preface section.



  1. Disconnect the negative battery cable.
  2. Remove the power steering pump. Refer to Power Steering Pump Replacement .
  3. Remove the A/C compressor. Refer to Air Conditioning Compressor Replacement : Diesel → V6 → 2.4L .
  4. Remove the rear A/C compressor mounting bracket bolts and the rear A/C compressor mounting bracket.


  5. Remove the accessory mounting bracket by removing the bolts.


  6. Disconnect the crankshaft position (CKP) sensor connector.
  7. Remove the CKP sensor retaining bolt.
  8. Gently rotate and remove the CKP sensor from the engine block.

Installation Procedure


  1. Install the CKP sensor into the engine block.

    2. Caution: Refer to Fastener Caution in the Preface section.

  2. Install the CKP sensor retaining bolt.

    3. Tighten
    Tighten the CKP sensor retaining bolt to 8 N·m (71 lb in).

  3. Connect the CKP sensor connector.


  4. Install the accessory mounting bracket with the bolts.

    5. Tighten
    Tighten the accessory mounting bracket bolts to 37 N·m (27 lb ft).



  5. Install the rear A/C mounting bracket.

    6. Tighten
    Tighten the rear A/C mounting bracket bolts to 35 N·m (26 lb ft).

  6. Install the A/C compressor. Refer to Air Conditioning Compressor Replacement : Diesel → V6 → 2.4L .
  7. Install the power steering pump. Refer to Power Steering Pump Replacement .
  8. Connect the negative battery cable.
