2nd Clutch Piston Travel Measurement

Special Tools

    • J-45166 Dial Indicator Extension Rod - 8 Inch
    • J-45200 Dial Indicator Set


  1. Install J-45166 extension into the fluid pump assembly bolt hole (1) indicated with the arrow.


  2. Assemble J-45200 indicator  (1) onto J-45166 extension  (2) and set the dial indicator to zero on the second clutch piston.

    3. Warning: Wear safety glasses when using compressed air in order to prevent eye injury.

  3. Apply compressed air into the second clutch fluid passage (B2) and measure the piston stroke. Be sure the piston moves smoothly.
  4. If the piston stroke is out of specifications, select another second clutch backing plate. Second clutch piston bore stroke: 1.10 - 1.50 mm (0.043 - 0.059 in).
