Air Conditioning Compressor Hose Replacement - Diesel

Special Tools

J 39400-A Halogen Leak Detector

Removal Procedures
  1. Recover the refrigerant. Refer to Refrigerant Recovery and Recharging .
  2. Remove the left headlamp. Refer to Headlamp Replacement : Long Body  → Short Body .


  3. Remove the evaporator outlet hose nut (1).
  4. Remove the evaporator outlet hose (2).
  5. Remove the air conditioning compressor hose bolt (3) from the electronic control module bracket.
  6. Remove the air conditioning compressor hose (4) from the electronic control module bracket.


  7. Remove the air conditioning compressor hose bolt (1) from the condenser.
  8. Remove the compressor hose (2) from the condenser.
  9. Raise and support the vehicle. Refer to Lifting and Jacking the Vehicle .


  10. Disconnect the electrical connector from the air conditioning refrigerant pressure sensor valve.
  11. Remove the compressor hose nut (1) at the compressor.
  12. Remove the compressor hose (2) from the compressor.
  13. Remove the compressor hose from the vehicle.
  14. Remove and discard the sealing washers from the compressor hose. Refer to Air Conditioning System Seal Replacement .

Installation Procedure


  1. Install new seal washers to the compressor hose. Refer to Air Conditioning System Seal Replacement .
  2. Install the compressor hose (2) to the compressor.

    3. Caution: Refer to Fastener Caution in the Preface section.

  3. Install the compressor hose nut (1) to the compressor and tighten to 22 N·m (16 lb ft).
  4. Connect the electrical connector to the air conditioning refrigerant pressure sensor valve.


  5. Lower the vehicle.
  6. Install the compressor hose (2) to the condenser.
  7. Install the air conditioning compressor hose bolt (1) to the condenser and tighten to 22 N·m (16 lb ft).


  8. Install the air conditioning compressor hose (4) to the electronic control module bracket.
  9. Install the air conditioning compressor hose bolt (3) to the electronic control module bracket and tighten to 20 N·m (15 lb ft)
  10. Install the evaporator outlet hose (2).
  11. Install the evaporator outlet hose nut (1) and tighten to 20 N·m (15 lb ft).
  12. Install the evaporator outlet hose to the air conditioning compressor hose. Refer to Evaporator Outlet Hose Replacement : V6 → Diesel → L4
  13. Install the left headlamp. Refer to Headlamp Replacement : Long Body  → Short Body .
  14. Recharge the A/C system. Refer to Refrigerant Recovery and Recharging .
  15. Test the affected A/C joints for leaks using J 39400-A.
